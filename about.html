Nigeria Tax Reform Act 2026 — plain English

Signed: June 26, 2025 — Effective: January 1, 2026.

The 2026 reform simplifies income tax for people and companies. Key points in everyday language:

  • Individuals: If you earn ₦800,000 or less a year — you pay no tax. Above that, your income is taxed using progressive bands (15%, 18%, 21%, 23%, 25%).
  • Businesses: Small companies (turnover ≤ ₦50M and fixed assets ≤ ₦250M) are exempt. Other companies pay 30% corporate tax plus a 4% development levy on profits. Big multinationals should ensure their effective tax rate is at least 15%.

This page is a plain-English summary for guidance. For official legal text and filing, consult FIRS and the Ministry of Finance.
