NB: Nigeria 2026 Tax Law Summary

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

Individual Tax (Pay As You Earn):

  • ₦0 – ₦800,000: 0% (Tax Exempt)
  • ₦800,001 – ₦3,000,000: 15%
  • ₦3,000,001 – ₦12,000,000: 18%
  • ₦12,000,001 – ₦25,000,000: 21%
  • ₦25,000,001 – ₦50,000,000: 23%
  • Above ₦50,000,000: 25%

Business Tax:

  • Turnover ≤ ₦50 million: Exempt
  • Turnover > ₦50 million: 30% Corporate Tax + 4% Development Levy
  • Large/multinational companies must meet a 15% minimum effective tax rate.
